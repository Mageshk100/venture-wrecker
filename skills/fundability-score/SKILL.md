# Fundability Score

Give the founder a clear, structured scorecard. Not vibes — actual dimensional analysis across the criteria VCs use to make decisions. This is the accountability mirror.

## How to Execute This Skill

Score the idea across these 6 dimensions, each out of 10. Be honest — a 6 is not bad, a 3 is a 3.

### Dimension 1: Problem Severity (0–10)
- Is this a hair-on-fire problem or a nice-to-have?
- Do people lose sleep over this?
- Are they already paying (time, money, workarounds) to solve it?

### Dimension 2: Market Size (0–10)
- Is the real SAM large enough to build a venture-scale business?
- Is the market growing?
- Can you reach $100M ARR in this market?

### Dimension 3: Competitive Moat (0–10)
- What makes this defensible in 3 years?
- Network effects, data moats, switching costs, proprietary tech, regulatory lock-in?
- Or is it just "we'll execute better"? (That's a 2.)

### Dimension 4: Business Model Clarity (0–10)
- Is it obvious how money flows?
- Are the unit economics directionally viable?
- Is there a clear path to gross margin > 60%?

### Dimension 5: Founder-Market Fit (0–10)
- Does the founder have an unfair advantage here?
- Domain expertise, distribution relationships, lived experience with the problem?
- Or are they an outsider with a Wikipedia understanding of the space?

### Dimension 6: Timing (0–10)
- What's the tailwind?
- Why is this the right moment — not 2 years ago, not 2 years from now?
- What macro trend, regulatory change, or technology unlock makes this possible today?
